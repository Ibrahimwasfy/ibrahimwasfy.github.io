01 / ABOUT

Engineering with intent.

I am an Automotive Engineering graduate from Helwan University — Mataria, shaped entirely by a lifelong obsession with motorsport. Every academic and professional choice I have made has been pointed in one direction: building faster, more dynamically capable machines.

My engineering foundation is built on rigorous first-principles work across vehicle dynamics and steering systems, backed by real competition results. I have led teams from Cairo to Doha, taking ownership of the complete development cycle — calculations, CAD, simulation, manufacturing, testing, and technical documentation — rather than contributing to isolated parts of the process.

I approach engineering with precision and persistence. Whether iterating through steering geometry to optimise Ackermann behaviour, or leading a multidisciplinary team under FSUK judging scrutiny, I bring the same detail-oriented mindset that motorsport demands.

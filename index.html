About Me

A motorsport enthusiast turned engineer. I've followed my passion for vehicles since childhood, ultimately earning a Bachelor of Automotive Engineering from Capital (Helwan) University — Mataria Branch, graduating 2026.

My hands-on journey spans two competitive engineering teams: Head of Vehicle Dynamics in Helwan Racing Team E-Rally, and currently Team Leader in HFS Racing Team, where I oversee the full vehicle development cycle — from engineering calculations and CAD design through simulation, manufacturing, assembly, and testing.

Beyond motorsport, I've gained real-world workshop experience across six industry internships, working with brands from Jaguar Land Rover to exotic marques like McLaren, Lamborghini, and Porsche.
